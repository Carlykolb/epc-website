Our Founding

ROOTED IN
SILICON VALLEY

Founded in October 2002 by Frank Taormina, Electrical Products and Control, Inc. (EPC, Inc.) is a privately owned company based in Silicon Valley. Frank's vision was clear: to make a meaningful impact on the San Jose electrical distribution industry, serving both local needs and a growing global market.

Over more than two decades, EPC has built a large and deeply loyal clientele — not through marketing, but through genuine commitment. Every order is treated like it's our first. We understand how hard it is to earn your trust, and how quickly it can be lost.

Today, EPC continues to grow while staying true to the principles that made us: quality products, competitive pricing, and customer service that goes the extra mile — every time.
